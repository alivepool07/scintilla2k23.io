About SCINTILLA

Welcome to our website

SCINTILLA is the science and linguistics exhibition organized by the Department of BASIC SCIENCE AND HUMANITIES, calling out science enthusiasts and linguists to present Models, Prototypes and Content driving cognitive communion, that tantalizes your imagination and piques your curiosity. SCINTILLA 2023 welcomes all to participate.
